Thorne

Warden of Solari

Backstory

Born under the blazing sun of Solari, Thorne was destined to guard the sacred Sunwell, a source of immense power said to be tied to the heart of the sun itself. With an unyielding sense of duty, Thorne has protected his people for centuries, wielding the power of solar flames and divine light. A stoic figure, he stands as the eternal protector of Solari, never allowing the darkness to spread. His armor gleams with the power of the Sun, and his shield bears the mark of the Solari itself.
